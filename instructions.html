Instructions

1. After you press any key, you will be directed to level 1 and one of the four buttons which appear on the screen will blink in random.

2. You should press the button which was blinked and if you fail to do so, your game is over and you have to restart your game.

3. When you press the correct button, you will be directed to next level in which another random button blinks.

4. Now you should press the button which was blinked in current level followed by the button blinked in previous level

5. Then in next level, you will have to press the buttons of level 1, 2 and 3 one after the other.

6. This forms a sequence of buttons which you have to memorize on your own and press the buttons in correct sequence in every level of the game.

7. Failing to do so in any level of the game will lead to erroneous input and you will have to play the game from the start.
